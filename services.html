Massage & Sports Therapy

Massage Therapy is an integral part of the RTSportsTherapy treatment and prevention programme, working alongside our rehabilitation services. We offer soft tissue massage therapy and sports therapy across Cheshire and Manchester at a location convenient to you. Clients can book 30–45 minute sessions or enquire about block bookings (see price list).

Sports Therapy & Deep Tissue Massage

Manual techniques are used to manipulate the soft tissues of the body to normalise tissue function as part of an ongoing treatment plan.

  • Recovery from injury
  • Support transitioning to a more active lifestyle
  • Preparation for physical or sporting goals
  • Applied by therapists experienced with professional rugby & Premier League athletes
