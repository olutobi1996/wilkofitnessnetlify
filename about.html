About Wilko Physiotherapy

Elite sports therapy, professional experience, and evidence-based rehabilitation delivered across Manchester.

Ryan Wilkinson BSc (Hons) Sports Therapy

Ryan graduated from the University of Central Lancashire in 2019 with a BSc (Hons) in Sports Therapy. Since graduating, he has worked within elite professional sporting environments including Everton FC and AFC Bournemouth U21’s, supporting athletes in achieving peak performance and successful injury rehabilitation.

Prior to this, Ryan owned and operated his own injury clinic working alongside a multidisciplinary team, while also completing medical internships with Everton FC, Wigan Warriors RFC and Leigh Miners RFC. Athletes of all abilities were treated to the same high professional standards — from grassroots level to elite performers.

Ryan believes in delivering individualised, evidence-based care focused on long-term results. Every treatment plan is tailored specifically to the patient’s needs, goals and lifestyle — whether recovering from injury, managing pain, or striving for improved athletic performance.
